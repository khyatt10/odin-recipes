Aztec Chicken with Sweet Potato-Corn Mash

Description

The Aztec Chicken with Sweet Potato-Corn Mash recipe is a delightful fusion of flavors that combines the ancient Aztec cuisine with modern culinary twists. This dish showcases tender grilled or roasted chicken marinated in a zesty blend of lime juice, garlic, cumin, and smoky paprika. The chicken is then served alongside a creamy and vibrant sweet potato-corn mash. The mash is made by combining roasted sweet potatoes, sweet corn kernels, and a touch of spices, resulting in a velvety and slightly sweet accompaniment that perfectly complements the savory Aztec chicken. The combination of the bold flavors from the chicken and the comforting sweetness of the mash creates a harmonious and satisfying meal.

What sets this recipe apart is the innovative use of sweet potatoes and corn to create a unique mash that adds an extra layer of richness to the Aztec chicken. The sweetness from the roasted sweet potatoes pairs beautifully with the natural sweetness of the corn, while the spices add a subtle depth of flavor. The vibrant colors and contrasting textures of the chicken and the sweet potato-corn mash create an appealing presentation that is as pleasing to the eye as it is to the palate. Whether enjoyed for a special occasion or as a comforting weeknight meal, the Aztec Chicken with Sweet Potato-Corn Mash recipe offers a delightful combination of ancient and modern flavors that will leave you craving more.

Ingredients

  • canola oil
  • 6 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon ancho chile powder
  • ½ tablespoon garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons Mexican-style hot sauce (such as Cholula®)
  • 6 (8 ounce) bone-in chicken breast halves

Directions

  1. Combine oil, honey, ancho chile powder, garlic, and hot sauce in a bowl; whisk together. Place chicken breasts in a large resealable plastic bag; pour in marinade and close bag. Marinate in refrigerator, 3 hours to overnight.
  2. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  3. Place sweet potatoes on a baking sheet. Place corn in a shallow baking pan. Brush with canola oil; season with salt and pepper. Cover pan with aluminum foil.
  4. Place both sweet potatoes and corn in the preheated oven. Bake until corn is roasted and brown, about 25 minutes. Continue baking until sweet potatoes are tender, about 45 minutes more.
  5. Allow corn and sweet potatoes to cool until easy to handle, about 20 minutes. Cut kernels from ears of corn; transfer half to a blender. Peel and cube sweet potatoes; add to the blender. Add cream; puree. Transfer mash to a bowl; stir in the remaining corn. Season with salt and pepper. Cover and keep warm.
  6. Preheat grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
  7. Remove chicken from bag and place on preheated grill; discard remaining marinade. Cook until no longer pink at the bone and the juices run clear, about 6 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted near the bone should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
  8. Cut chicken breasts into 3 equal pieces; serve on top of sweet potato-corn mash.
